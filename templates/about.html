How We Started

Our Journey

Suja Consultancies and HR Consultancy is a start-up platform for talented individuals, established in 2022 in Kerala. We aim to exceed our customers' and clients' expectations by offering expert consultation, services, and solutions in human resource policies, focusing on emerging markets.

Our Reach

With a commitment to serving all over India, Suja Consultancies aims to be a leading talent acquisition firm, providing comprehensive HR solutions across various domains to enhance organizational growth.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to the fair selection and development of a diverse workforce, aiming to build brighter futures by turning ideas into action and sparking positive change.

Embracing Remote Work

We are transitioning to a 100% remote process, collaborating with remote recruiters and utilizing crowd staffing, the most effective recruitment method in the market, to hire the right talent for the right purpose.

Client-Centric Approach

We cater to a wide range of staffing needs, from top management positions to junior-level hiring, offering tailored packages to suit businesses of all sizes, without compromising on quality but at affordable prices.

  Our Vision

To emerge as a preferred partner in Consulting, Talent Acquisition, and HR Practice, providing quality HR solutions that add value to our clients and customers .

  Our Mission

To provide HR consulting for emerging companies, committed to the fair selection and development of a diverse workforce, paving the way for change in the industry.
