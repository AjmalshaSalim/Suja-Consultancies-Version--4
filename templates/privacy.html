Privacy and Policy

At Suja Consultancies & HR Services, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or engage with our recruitment and HR services.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We may collect personal information such as your name, contact details, resume, and professional history when you submit an inquiry or apply for job opportunities through our website.

b. Automatically Collected Information

We may gather non-personal information, including IP addresses, browser type, and device information, to enhance the user experience and improve our services.

2. Use of Information

a. Recruitment and HR Services

Personal information is collected to facilitate the recruitment process, assess your suitability for job opportunities, and connect you with potential employers.

b. Communication

We may use your contact information to respond to inquiries, provide updates on job applications, and share relevant career opportunities.

c. Analytics

Non-personal information may be used for statistical analysis, website optimization, and to enhance our overall service quality.

3. Data Security

a. Security Measures

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

b. Confidentiality

Our staff is trained to handle personal information with confidentiality, and access is restricted to those who require it for legitimate business purposes

4. Third-Party Disclosure

a. Authorized Partners

We may share your personal information with authorized third parties, such as our partner companies involved in the recruitment process.

b. Legal Requirements

We may disclose information in response to legal requests or when required by law to protect our rights, property, or safety

5. Your Choices

a. Access and Correction

You have the right to access, correct, or update your personal information. Please contact us if you wish to make changes

You can opt-out of receiving communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in our emails

a. Use of Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

10. Additional/Paid Services for Candidates

Candidate placement Fees

Suja Consultancies HR Consultancy never ask for payments/fees from candidates for doing a placement. Suja Consultancies Recruitment HR Consultancy shall not be responsible for any kind of transactions held between the job seeker and any Employer/Consultant.

  • Placement Charges will be 8.33%* of the selected candidate’s gross annual income* for junior level positions (up to the level of Assistant and Executive level.)
  • 8.5% of the selected candidate’s gross annual income* for senior level (From the level of Admin & above.)
  • PLACEMENT SERVICES will be one time flat rate of 8.33%* of the annual cost of the company, indicated in the offer letter to the recruited candidate including salary, allowance, benefits, transportation and perquisites such as LTA, Bonus, and Medical etc.

  • Depending upon the volume of the business the placement services charges can be negotiated

  • Guarantee Free substitution of personnel once registered, within Three months of employment if he/ she leaves the job
