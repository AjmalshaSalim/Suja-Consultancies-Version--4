Interview Tips

Do you have your first interview scheduled and don’t know how to prepare for it? Here, at Suja Consultancies, find out the best interview tips for freshers that will help you crack the interview.

“Getting the First Opportunity is Tough, but utilizing the opportunity is more crucial!”

Facing an interview is always stressful and if you are a fresher it can also trigger anxiety! While on one hand, you are excited to enter the corporate world, on the other hand, there is a constant fear of being rejected. However, rather than being overwhelmed with negative thoughts, it’s better to be prepared for the interview. Apart from this, you might not know from where you can prepare for the interview and what steps to follow for cracking the interview of your dream company.

What is an Interview?

An interview is typically a conversation between a job applicant and a representative of the employer. This is generally conducted to assess the qualities of the applicant to make sure that he/she is suitable for that particular job role. Here it is very important to note that an interview doesn’t only help the employer find a suitable candidate but it also helps the applicant decide whether the job is a good fit for his/her qualifications. Therefore, every organization makes a crucial decision of hiring a candidate by the way of an interview.

    You’ve almost made it!

    After an intense couple of months of sending your CV to different companies that you hope to work for, finally, you’ve received a call back that your resume has been shortlisted, and you’ve been invited for an interview with HR.

    Generally, it can be tough for candidates to know what to expect during HR interview questions for freshers. These days, the interview style is changing rapidly. Gone are the days when your technical skill set dominated the conversation. It’s all about you as a person now, but don’t worry because we’ve got your back!

    A typical job interview lasts between 45 minutes and 1 hour. For this assessment, the Human Resource (HR) team of any organization has a set of questions to ask all the interview candidates to understand their aspirations. There are key questions that hiring managers will ask you during your job interview. These questions could vary depending on the type of job you’re applying for, but there are some common questions that are asked by most interviewers during the newest hire process.

    In the HR round of a job interview, the questions are designed to see how you could handle certain situations at work. The aim is to see how your past experiences translate into what you will do in future roles. Here’s a guide to some of the most common HR interview questions and answers.

    We’ll look at the top HR interview questions and answers for freshers to prepare you for any question a potential employer might ask.

    Basic Questions
    • Q1.Hello How Are You Today?

      • This is more of a “make yourself comfortable” statements. Answer with a pleasant smile. If you had any trouble during the day, don’t hesitate to mention it. But make sure you express it in a positive note.

      Answer :

      “I am very well today sir, thank you”.
      “I am very well today sir, just that the traffic here is bit of a hassle. But I made sure, I start a bit early to beat it.”

    • Q2.Tell me a little about yourself

      • This is one of the first HR interview questions for freshers that the interviewer asks. This question aims to understand some background about you and your dreams and contextualize it with the company values.Tell each and every top quality, which matters for them and the explanations are apart from the facts described in resume.

      Answer :

      First of all, I would like to thank you and ____________ (company) for giving me this opportunity to present myself in front of you and accept my candidature.

      Coming to myself, As you know my name is _________, which means __________. I have humble beginnings from _______ (town/city) and grew up understanding the importance of working hard. My father was a ____________ and always taught me different lessons about the true nature of goodwill and hard work.

      I began showing interest in _________ (sales/marketing/etc) during my school years. Studying _______ opened my world to the immense possibilities in this field, and I immediately got excited to finish my schooling and enter this work.

      Apart from this, I have a special interest in __________ (hobby) and have successfully participated in various competitions where I have shown my talent. My interest in sports manifested in me playing ________ (football/cricket) throughout my childhood and am an avid supporter of _______ (sports team)

    • Q3.How long can you commit to work with us?

      • This is another one of the many HR interview questions for freshers that most people fumble at. A Company will want to appoint you if you can add some valuable point to it.However, it is extremely easy to understand the answer to such types of hr interview questions for freshers. They aim to understand how you would fit into the company, so talking about the company’s ethos and its values is a good start.So, focus on your capabilities and how they can be valuable for the position you are being interviewed for.

      Apart from this, many freshers think that because they have no work experience, no company will be willing to hire them. However, being fresher is a strength you have. You are like a blank canvas ready to start your masterpiece. So, you should sell yourself like that.tell them the unique factors which are valuable for their company & also positive factor in profile for which you are applying.

      Answer :

      Give the answer of this question such as, I like new challenges and an opportunity to grow. As long I keep getting these, I don’t think I’ll need to switch over. I’d like to believe that this connection can last for many years. However, I haven’t set a time period restrict as such.
